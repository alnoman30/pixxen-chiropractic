Nine reasons most chiropractic websites lose patients researching back pain

Chiropractic practices compete in a category where every site uses the same spinal-diagram hero and the same vague service descriptions. Most chiropractic sites we audit are leaking qualified new-patient inquiries in ways the doctor hasn't noticed yet.

No online new-patient intake or appointment booking

Patients in pain want to book tonight, not call tomorrow. Sites without online booking lose the after-hours pain-driven prospect to the practice that has it.

Generic Services page where condition-specific pages should live

Lower back pain, neck pain, headaches, sciatica, herniated disc, sports injury — each is a separate search intent with a different patient mindset. One Services page collapses all of it.

Doctor credentials missing or hidden

DC degree, school of graduation, technique certifications (Gonstead, Diversified, Activator, Thompson, ART, Webster for prenatal), years of experience — trust signals patients actively look for.s

No insurance acceptance information

Patients shop on insurance acceptance. Sites that surface Aetna, Cigna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, UnitedHealthcare, Medicare-acceptance information win the insured-patient segment.

First-visit pricing transparency missing

New patients shop on first-visit cost. $49 first visit, $79 with X-ray, $129 comprehensive evaluation — published pricing qualifies serious calls and filters tire-kickers.

No new patient onboarding pathway

First-time chiropractic patients do not know what to expect. Online intake → first-visit exam → X-ray (if needed) → treatment plan → adjustment answers the silent objection.

Technique-specific positioning missing

Gonstead, Diversified, Activator, Thompson, ART, NUCCA — technique specialization is a search differentiator. Generic adjustments wording loses to specific-technique positioning.

Patient stories without specifics

Pain-free in three visits converts no one. Six months of sciatica, MRI showed herniated L4-L5, 11 visits over 8 weeks, walking the dog again converts the next patient with the same condition.

Is the $999 price really all-in?

Yes. Logo, branding, chiropractic website, SEO content, on-page SEO setup, Google Business Profile optimization, and 30 days of post-launch support. Practice management software (ChiroTouch, ChiroSpring, Genesis) subscriptions are your existing tools.

Will my practice rank #1 for 'chiropractor [my city]'?

No one can guarantee a #1 Google ranking, but we build your website and SEO foundation to maximize your chances of ranking for “chiropractor [city]” through local SEO, optimized service pages, Google Business Profile improvements, and targeted content designed to attract new patients.

Do you integrate with ChiroTouch, ChiroSpring, Genesis, ChiroFusion?

Yes. We can connect your website with your existing practice management tools like ChiroTouch, ChiroSpring, Genesis, and ChiroFusion for patient intake, appointment requests, and workflow needs. Your software subscriptions and account setup remain yours, and we work around your existing systems.

How do you handle state chiropractic board advertising compliance?

We design with chiropractic advertising guidelines in mind, avoiding misleading claims, unsupported guarantees, and non-compliant language. We structure your website content, testimonials, services, and marketing messaging to align with your state board requirements, while recommending final review by your practice or legal advisor for state-specific compliance.

What if I already have a site on Wix, Squarespace, or an old WordPress build?

We can work with your existing Wix, Squarespace, or WordPress site. We’ll review what you currently have, preserve any valuable SEO assets, and recommend whether to rebuild, migrate, or improve the existing website based on your goals.

Do you do ongoing SEO or content writing after launch?

Yes. We offer ongoing SEO and content support after launch, including local SEO improvements, new service pages, blog content, optimization updates, and strategies designed to help your practice continue growing in search visibility over time.

Can you build sites for multi-doctor practices?

Yes. We build websites for multi-doctor practices with dedicated provider profiles, service pages, location pages, team-focused messaging, and scalable structures designed to support multiple practitioners and specialties.

How do you handle HIPAA on the website?

We design websites with HIPAA considerations in mind by avoiding the collection or storage of protected health information (PHI) through non-compliant forms or tools. When patient data collection is needed, we recommend using HIPAA-compliant platforms and integrations, with proper security settings and agreements in place. Practice owners should also review their specific compliance requirements with their HIPAA advisor.

What happens after the 30-day support window?

After the 30-day support window, your website remains fully yours. You can continue managing it independently or choose ongoing support for updates, SEO, content improvements, and future website needs.

How does this compare to chiropractic-specific marketing platforms (ChiroBuilders, Perfect Patients)?

We provide a custom, conversion-focused website built around your practice, niche, and goals, rather than a one-size-fits-all platform. You can still use your preferred chiropractic marketing tools alongside it.
